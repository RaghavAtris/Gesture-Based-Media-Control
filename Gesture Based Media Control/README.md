# Gesture Based Volume Control

A programme that uses hand gestures to control media of your system. Your media can be controlled by flashing numbers from 1 to 5.

## No.1
Fast Forward 5 seconds

## No.2
Rewind 5 seconds

## No.3
Increase Volume

## No.4
Decrease Volume

## No.5
Play & Pause
